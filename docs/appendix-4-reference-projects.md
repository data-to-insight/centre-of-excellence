# <span style="color:dodgerblue">Appendix 4: Reference projects</span>

We maintain a list of reference projects we’ve learned from to arrive at our current understanding. We recognise that this list is by no means a comprehensive representation of the data and digital activity or expertise in the sector, but we plan to share it to help colleagues identify other important reference points for our design work, as part of our engagement, and to serve as a resource for future reference.

During this project, we’re working to publish a prototype map of the work happening in the sector, using these projects as a base, and welcoming contributions from colleagues across the sector. We hope that this will become an important and sustainable resource for colleagues.
